02 About

Designing at the intersection of space & computation

I'm an architect with 3+ years of practice across India and the UAE, trained at Morphogenesis (Delhi), SHAPE Architecture & Research (UAE), and DNEG (Mumbai) — spanning sustainable architecture, parametric design, and spatial visualisation for world-class VFX productions.

Currently pursuing a Master in AI for Architecture & the Built Environment at IAAC, Barcelona, where my work sits at the intersection of artificial intelligence and the built environment. My research was published at ANNSIM 2025 through the Building Analysis Tool — an AI-driven accessibility analyser for healthcare architecture.
