02 — About

Designing at the intersection
of space & computation

Background

I'm an architect with 3+ years of practice across India and the UAE, trained at Morphogenesis (Delhi), SHAPE Architecture & Research (UAE), and DNEG (Mumbai) — spanning sustainable architecture, parametric design, and spatial visualisation for world-class VFX productions.

Research

Currently pursuing a Master in City & Technology at IAAC, Barcelona, where my work sits at the intersection of artificial intelligence and built environment. My research has been published at ANNSIM 2025 (Society of Modelling & Simulation International) through the Building Analysis Tool — an AI-driven accessibility analyser for healthcare architecture.

Projects

My studio work explores how AI can augment architectural decision-making at every scale — from individual buildings to entire cities. Recent projects include a structural analysis tool that runs finite element analysis on any 3D model at concept stage; a 500-agent urban simulation where each agent reasons in natural language powered by an LLM; and a pedestrian simulation platform for validating exhibition layouts before construction.

Other Work

At the AEC TECH Hackathon 2025 at BIG's Barcelona office, our team ZONO_NAUTS used graph neural networks to predict urban growth patterns and voxelised the results into an explorable Minecraft-style city model. A separate civic platform, AI4ALL Decision, makes 23 years of global satellite land-cover data legible to non-experts through an interactive split-screen map.
