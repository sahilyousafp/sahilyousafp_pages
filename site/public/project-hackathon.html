ZONO_NAUTS Urban Growth GNN

Built in 48 hours at AEC TECH Hackathon 2025 at BIG's Barcelona office. Graph neural networks predict city growth patterns and Minecraft-style voxelisation makes the predictions tangible to non-experts.

Overview+

Urban development meets simulation, meets play

City growth models are notoriously opaque — driven by equations planners and citizens can't interpret. The ZONO_NAUTS challenge was: how do you make urban prediction legible?

First, a Graph Neural Network trained on Barcelona's urban grid to predict zoning transitions, land use dominance, and new development zones — patterns invisible to traditional analysis.

Second, a Minecraft-style voxelisation pipeline that converts the city grid into a spatial format anyone can explore. Urban development becomes a world you can walk through.

Workflow+

Modular pipeline

01

OSMnx Extraction

Building footprints, land use polygons, and POIs extracted from OpenStreetMap for any target city.

02

Grid-to-Graph

A 250×250m urban grid is converted to a graph format for GNN training, preserving spatial relationships.

03

GNN Training

Graph Neural Network predicts zoning transitions and land use dominance across urban nodes.

04

Voxelisation

3D city blocks converted to .schematic format — making urban predictions interactive and spatially immersive.
